frequently asked questions

General

What is the distance to the beach?

The beach is just steps away!"

Is there a balcony/terrace?

Yes, our apartment have a private balcony/terrace.

Does the apartment have air conditioning?

Absolutely! The apartment comes equipped with air conditioning to ensure your comfort during your stay. You can relax and enjoy the calming sounds of the waves crashing nearby

Accommodation

How many people can the apartment accommodate?

The apartment comfortably accommodates a maximum of 4 guests. It features a cozy bedroom with queen-size bed and a comfortable sofa bed in the living area, perfect for additional guests.

What bedding is included?

To ensure a comfortable stay from the moment you arrive, we provide all the essential bedding for your stay:
  • Sheets: Freshly laundered sheets will be on the bed(s) upon your arrival.
  • Towels: We provide bath towels and hand towels for each guest.
  • Blankets: An extra blanket will be available in the closet or linen cupboard for additional warmth if needed.

Is there Wi-Fi access?

Free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the apartment

Policies

What are the check-in and check-out times?

  • Check-in: 14:00 in the afternoon. This allows our cleaning staff ample time to prepare the apartment for your arrival after the previous guests depart.
  • Check-out: 11:00 in the morning. This allows us to clean and prepare the apartment for incoming guests.
  • Early Check-In and Late Check-Out: We understand that travel plans can sometimes be unpredictable. While we can't guarantee early check-in or late check-out due to cleaning schedules, we recommend letting us know your arrival and departure times in advance. This allows us to see if accommodating your request might be possible.

Is the apartment pet-friendly?

We understand you might like to bring your furry friend along! While we love pets, our apartment can only accommodate small pets. For the comfort of all guests and to protect the furnishings, we kindly ask that only pets considered small (e.g., weighing under 10 kg) are allowed.

What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is in place to ensure fairness for both guests and the property. You can find the full details of our cancellation policy [link to cancellation policy details on your website].

Additional Services

Are there any nearby restaurants or shops?

Absolutely! You'll be spoiled for choice with a variety of restaurants and shops all within easy reach of the apartment.
  • Dining: For those craving a casual meal, there are pizzerias, gyro stands, and burger joints nearby. If you're looking for fresh seafood, you won't be disappointed either.
  • Shopping: From everyday essentials to souvenirs, you'll find all kinds of shops and markets within walking distance.

What are the local attractions?

Asprovalta offers a wealth of exciting things to see and do, whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, or cultural immersion. To help you plan your itinerary, we've curated a dedicated attractions page on our website. You'll find information on a variety of options, including:
  • Relaxation and Beaches: Soak up the sun on the beautiful Asprovalta beach, just steps away from the apartment.
  • Nature and Outdoors: Explore scenic walking trails, rent a bike, or take a day trip to stunning natural wonders like Lake Volvi or the Varvara Waterfalls.
  • History and Culture: Immerse yourself in local traditions at the Asprovalta Folklore Museum, visit historic monasteries, or embark on day trips to ancient cities and archaeological sites.
