Our Apartment

A cozy apartment by the sea in Asprovalta, Greece

Location

Our apartment is located in the heart of Asprovalta, a charming seaside town in Greece. You can walk to the beach in less than a minute, and enjoy the sun, the sand, and the sound of the waves. Our apartment is the ideal place to experience the best of Asprovalta.

Attractions

Asprovalta is a charming seaside town in Greece, with a rich history, culture, and natural beauty. You can visit the ancient ruins of Stagira, the birthplace of Aristotle, or the impressive Lion of Amphipolis. You can also enjoy spectacular Varvara Waterfalls, a hidden gem in the forest.

Remote Work

Our apartment is suitable for digital nomads and remote workers who want to live and work in Greece. We have super fast internet and essential equipment for your work needs. You can also enjoy the beach, the town, and the friendly locals in Asprovalta.

Amenities

Our apartment offers you a range of amenities to make your stay more comfortable and enjoyable. You can benefit from our free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and storage space. You can also relax on our private balcony, cook in our fully equipped kitchen. Our apartment has everything!
