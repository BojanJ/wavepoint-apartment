Welcome to your home away from home in Asprovalta, Greece

Welcome to Wave Point Apartment

We're thrilled to have you as our guest! This house manual contains all the information you need to make your stay comfortable and enjoyable. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Check-in and Check-out

  • Check-in: 3:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM
  • Late check-in: Please contact us in advance
  • Keys: Located in a lockbox - code will be sent before arrival

Wi-Fi Information

  • Network: Wave Point Apartment
  • Password: Asprovalta2024
  • Fast internet suitable for remote work

Kitchen and Appliances

The kitchen is fully equipped for your cooking needs:

  • Refrigerator with freezer
  • Electric stove and oven
  • Coffee maker and kettle
  • Microwave
  • Dishware, cookware, and utensils
  • Basic spices and condiments provided

Air Conditioning and Heating

  • Remote controls are located on the coffee table
  • For energy efficiency, please turn off when leaving
  • Recommended temperature: 22-25°C

Beach and Location

  • Beach is less than 1 minute walk from the apartment
  • Supermarket and restaurants within walking distance
  • Public parking available nearby

House Rules

  • No smoking inside the apartment
  • No parties or loud music after 10 PM
  • Maximum occupancy: 4 guests
  • Please keep the apartment clean and tidy
  • Report any damages immediately

Emergency Information

  • Emergency Services: 112
  • Host Contact: +30 694 814 5850
  • Local Hospital: Asprovalta Health Center
  • Police: Asprovalta Police Station

Nearby Attractions

  • Stagira (Aristotle's birthplace): 30 minutes drive
  • Varvara Waterfalls: 45 minutes drive

  • Thessaloniki: 1.5 hours drive
